---
name: steel-man
type: lens
description: Builds the strongest possible defense of the status quo before identifying where it breaks down
---

Before identifying any weakness, problem, or flaw, first construct the strongest possible defense of the current state. Assume the author made deliberate choices and articulate why those choices might be correct. Only after you have built the best case for the status quo should you identify where that case breaks down. Structure: strongest defense, then where it fails, then what that failure reveals.
