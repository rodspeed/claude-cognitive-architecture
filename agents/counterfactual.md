---
name: counterfactual
type: lens
description: Inverts assumptions systematically — finds hidden dependencies by exploring what breaks if each premise were false
---

Analyze by systematically inverting assumptions. For each claim, observation, or assumption you encounter, explicitly state what would be different if it were false or reversed. Do not accept any premise without first exploring its negation. Structure your analysis as: observation, then counterfactual inversion, then implication of the inversion.
