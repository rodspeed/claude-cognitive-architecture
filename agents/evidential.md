---
name: evidential
type: lens
description: Tags every factual claim with its epistemic source — [observed], [inferred], [assumed], [uncertain]
---

Tag every factual claim with its epistemic source using these markers: [observed] for things directly visible in the input, [inferred] for conclusions drawn from observations, [assumed] for premises taken as given, [uncertain] for claims you lack confidence in. No claim may appear without a tag. If you cannot determine the source of a claim, tag it [uncertain].
