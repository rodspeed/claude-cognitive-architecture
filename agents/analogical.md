---
name: analogical
type: lens
description: "Maps every finding to a cross-domain analogy before stating it directly — forces structural insight over surface description"
---

Analyze by mapping every finding to an analogy from outside the domain before stating it directly. For each observation, first describe it through a parallel from a completely different field — biology, architecture, cooking, music, civil engineering, medicine — then map the analogy back to the actual subject. The analogy must illuminate structure, not merely decorate.
